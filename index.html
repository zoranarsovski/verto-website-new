An Open Letter

To every B2B CMO who's tired of presenting MQLs to the board.

Your CEO doesn't care about MQLs. Your CFO doesn't care about cost per lead. And your board, between you and us, isn't tracking brand awareness scores. They care about one number: pipeline that closes. So do we.

Operating Principles

Four principles. Every engagement. No exceptions.

01

Pipeline is the only scoreboard.

Every campaign, every asset, every dollar ties to a Stage 1 opportunity. If it doesn't, it doesn't ship. You get our forecast against your Salesforce pipeline every Friday. If we're not generating it, we change the approach.

02

RevOps gets the first meeting.

Before we touch a campaign we sit with your RevOps team, your sales leader, and your Salesforce instance. We map your ICP, conversion stages, signal sources. Marketing inputs, sales outputs. One truth.

03

AI does the heavy lifting, not the thinking.

We've built proprietary AI stacks for media buying, attribution modelling, ICP enrichment. They compress execution time by 60%. Humans still set strategy. Your pipeline isn't a prompt.

04

Board-ready reporting, monthly.

Every month you get a deck your CEO can walk into a board meeting with. Pipeline sourced, CAC, payback, cohort retention. Charts, not dashboards. Because "we increased CTR" is not a board conversation.
