The system

How the system works.

Most agencies optimize each channel in isolation. We sequence them. The three layers feed each other — and the whole stack lifts when any one of them gets stronger.

01 · Generate

Paid Social.

ICP awareness. Reaching the buyer group before they search. Firmographic precision — actual titles, actual companies — not generic interests.

02 · Harvest

The fastest pipeline channel. Captures hand-raisers already in-market. The bid algorithm learns from CRM signals, not form-fills.

03 · Compound

Organic engine. Built for both Google and AI search. Lowers CAC across Generate and Harvest over time.

The 90-day pilot

What ninety days of inbound looks like.

A Verto inbound pilot is a fixed-fee, fixed-scope engagement. The point isn't to test ideas. It's to prove pipeline contribution, channel by channel, before any long-term commitment.

DAY 0–30

Foundation.

1st-party data activation deployed. Server-side GTM live. CRM-to-ad-platform feedback loop built. ICP audience segmentation activated on LinkedIn. Search campaign infrastructure rebuilt around offline conversion data.

DAY 30–60

Activation.

LinkedIn awareness layer at frequency. Paid Search bid algorithm trained on real buyer signals. SEO/AEO technical cleanup complete. First content assets published targeting BOF and transactional queries.

DAY 60–90

Proof.

Pipeline-attributed opportunities tracked end-to-end. Channel contribution reports delivered. CFO-grade attribution model live. Ranking movement on target queries. Awareness layer warm enough for Search to compound from.

What makes it work

Behind the channels: Pipeline Intelligence.

The numbers above don't come from creative or clever bidding. They come from feeding deal-stage signals back to Google, LinkedIn, and Microsoft — so the bid algorithm optimizes for who buys, not who fills forms. That's the work the Pipeline Intelligence team does on every inbound engagement.
